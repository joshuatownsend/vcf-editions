  • vSAN Edition
  • Storage Policy-Based ManagementAllow common management across storage tiers and dynamic SLA automation via policy-driven control plane. Policies can be applied on a per-VM level and adjusted on the fly. No LUNs or RAID configurations.
  • vSAN Snapshots & ClonesHighly efficient VM-centric snapshots and clones with support for up to 32 snapshots or clones per VM and 16K snapshots or clones per cluster, leveraging the unique vSAN File System.
  • Rack AwarenessvSAN fault domains provide the ability to tolerate rack failures in addition to disk, network, and host failures.
  • Replication (5 min RPO)Use vSphere replication to asynchronously replicate VMs across sites based on configurable schedules of up to 5 minutes RPO (unique to vSAN).
  • Software ChecksumEnd-to-end software checksum of data enables automatic detection and resolution of silent disk errors and ensures data integrity.
  • All-Flash SupportCreate an all-flash architecture delivering consistent, predictable performance with up to 100K IOPS/Host and sub-millisecond response times.
  • Block Access (iSCSI)Expand to new, non-virtualized workloads with iSCSI access.
  • QoS - IOPS LimitLimit IOPS consumption per VM to better manage performance SLAs for different workloads. Eliminates “noisy neighbor” issues.
  • Inline Deduplication & Compression (All-Flash Only)Optimized for all-flash systems. Reduce space usage by up to 7x, providing attractive $/GB for a hyperconverged all-flash system.
  • Erasure Coding - RAID-5/6 (All-Flash Only)Single parity protection (RAID-5) from a single failure and double parity protection (RAID-6) from two simultaneous failures. Dynamically change between RAID protection levels through policies and increase storage utilization by up to 2x.
  • vRealize Operations within vCenterSingle pane of glass to monitor and control HCI environment.
  • Stretched Cluster with Local Failure ProtectionProvides protection against both site failures and local component failures. vSAN ensures high performance by utilizing the local site copy.
  • Data-at-Rest EncryptionCluster-level encryption for data stored on any persistent media.
